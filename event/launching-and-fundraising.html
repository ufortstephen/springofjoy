Launching and Fundraising Event of Spring of Joy for Widows Foundation

The Spring of Joy for Widows Foundation celebrated a momentous occasion on August 17th, 2024, with its highly anticipated launch and fundraising event. Held in the heart of the city, the event brought together a wide array of attendees, including philanthropists, community leaders, influencers, and local businesses, all united by a common cause: to support and empower widows in need.

One of the most impactful parts of the event was a series of heartfelt testimonials from widows who have already benefited from the foundation’s pilot programs. These women courageously shared their personal stories of loss, resilience, and transformation, illustrating how the foundation’s resources have helped them rebuild their lives. Their stories were a powerful reminder of the importance of the foundation’s mission and left many in the audience deeply moved.

Guests enjoyed a series of inspiring performances, interactive sessions, and opportunities to connect with the foundation’s leadership. The fundraising efforts exceeded expectations, with generous contributions from attendees and partners that will help sustain essential programs for widows in need. The event was a remarkable celebration of hope, solidarity, and the shared commitment to making a difference in the lives of widows.

With the founder of Little Saints Orphanage( Rev. Mrs Dele George)and her board of trustees
With the church family- RCCG Christ Disciples Parish, Surulere Lagos.
The Senior Pastor of RCCG Christ Disciples Parish - Pastor Richard Umoren
Outreach Coordinator of Spring of Joy for Widows Foundation - Apostle Paul Nwankwo

Our NGO is committed to continuing this vital work, ensuring that widows in our community feel valued, supported, and empowered to thrive despite life's challenges.
